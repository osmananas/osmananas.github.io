01 Research Focus
Earthquake Cycle Geodesy
Studying interseismic strain accumulation, coseismic rupture, and postseismic relaxation using geodetic observations to constrain fault locking and slip models across the earthquake cycle.
InSAR & Surface Deformation
Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar analysis using Sentinel-1 for mapping crustal deformation, urban subsidence, and fault surface rupture at millimeter-scale precision.
GNSS & Satellite Geodesy
Precise positioning through multi-constellation GNSS, time series analysis for detecting secular and transient deformation signals, and space weather effects on positioning accuracy.
Geoid Modelling
Determination of regional gravimetric geoid models using least-squares collocation, evaluation of global geopotential models against ground truth GNSS/levelling and gravity data.
Land Subsidence Monitoring
Multi-temporal InSAR analysis of urban land surface deformation coupled with land use / land cover change analysis, applied to cities across Indonesia and East Africa.
Geodetic Reference Systems
Evaluation and modernization of national geodetic reference frames; assessment of high-degree global geopotential models over Sudan using GPS/levelling benchmark data.
